04 Technologies

Technologies we use

The technology stack we use in e-commerce store development.

  • Shopify

    We use the Shopify & Shopify Plus platform to develop online stores. With Shopify's extensive API and app selection, we're able to tailor your store to your customers' needs.

  • Ruby on Rails

    With the right Ruby on Rails set-up, we can create any custom script linking Shopify to other technological solutions, including ERP systems, delivery providers or payment methods.

  • Analytics software

    To ensure proper UX/UI design, we use analytical software, such as Hotjar or Heap Analytics, to measure data and use it to introduce A/B testing and optimize your store's design.
