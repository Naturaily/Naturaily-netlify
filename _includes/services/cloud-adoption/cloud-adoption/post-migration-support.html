Post-migration support

Migration to the cloud is a means, not a goal. This is why all the things happening after cloud migration are vital for successful digital transformation. When your cloud migration is complete, we offer maintenance services to ensure the stability and security of all your data.

When your cloud migration is complete, we offer maintenance services to ensure the stability and security of all your data. We operate based on a Service Level Agreement (SLA). Our DevOps team can be available in different time variants and offer various response times - all to help you get the best of your cloud solutions.

Our post-migration support covers:

  • Performance monitoring

    We will track and report application bottlenecks, as well as application availability and responsiveness.

  • Utilization monitoring

    We will manage and review all operational processes in your cloud infrastructure to give you full control over resource usage.

  • Scaling the cloud

    We will measure consumption levels and make sure that your cloud resources keep up with the business needs.
