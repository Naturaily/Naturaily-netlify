New website for one of the biggest Polish banks
In September 2018 Eurobank released a new version of their website. The project was a collaborative effort of several teams of specialists.

Among them was Naturaily's content-design team responsible for creating the information architecture of the website, as well as filling it with the right content and creating ready-to-implement CMS templates.

The content for over 130 product and informative pages was prepared and implemented in just two months, fully in line with the communication strategy established at the beginning of the cooperation.
What mattered to us most was for the user not to have any trouble with understanding the texts, what can be hard in the banking industry. Naturaily made it easy to accomplish. They proposed an effective cooperation process, created a specific roadmap that considered tasks being delivered by various teams: an internal Eurobank team, the content project team and the implementation team. Their engagement was way beyond the agreed responsibility line! I wish everyone with a demanding project such a pro-active team.

Defina Kaczalko

Marketing Manager, Euro Bank S.A.
