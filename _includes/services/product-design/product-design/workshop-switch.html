Workshops

Product Design Workshop is the best way to clarify your vision, get the answers to crucial questions, and determine the final product you want to create.

We will adapt proven methodologies (such as event storming and business model canvas) to achieve these goals.

All conclusions will be written down in the post-workshop report, including documentation and wireframes, which will be an excellent starting point in the development process.

These five days of hard work will surely pay off, as we will identify all the weak points of the project, plan further work and carefully estimate the budget.
