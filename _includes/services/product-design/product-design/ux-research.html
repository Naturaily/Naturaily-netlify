UX Research

As we said before knowing is the key to success not presuming. That’s why we ask questions, we take notes, we try to know as much as possible about the target audience.

Research is a powerful tool for helping us identify and either prove or disprove our assumptions. It also helps us recognize the needs and goals of future users, as well as their mental model, consequently enabling us in creating better products and services.

There are many research methodologies available, from in-person interviews to unmoderated A/B tests. We choose the ones that turn out to be the most appropriate in a given situation to understand the ‘how’ and the ‘why’.

Thanks to UX research we can design with the end-user in mind.
