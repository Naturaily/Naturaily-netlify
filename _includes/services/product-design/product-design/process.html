Product design process

When designing a product, we have to do it based on human nature, not what we think is human nature. Reliable and properly interpreted data is the best basis in the design process.

An invaluable stage of this creative process is a five-day series of interactive workshops when we take the product from its initial design idea through to prototyping and user testing.

These few days of intensive work results in the knowledge base necessary to design the right product.
