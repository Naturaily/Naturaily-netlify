Case study

Product design and development for a book sharing platform

Snipsl is a platform for sharing books and stories for both readers and authors. Our goal was to let the authors interact with their readers better - we achieved that by adding a survey system and letting users ‘heart’ the stories and easily share them on social media.

This successful cooperation has resulted in over 5,000 active users and 900 books published by 500 authors.
