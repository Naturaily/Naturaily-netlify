Analysis

This is the initial stage of the product design process that helps to define problems that the designed product may be a solution to. By asking some basic questions, like:

  • what problem are we trying to solve?
  • who has that problem?
  • what do we want to accomplish?

we are getting closer to understanding the user experience of a product as a whole (not only its look & feel) and understanding the product's context for existence.

Based on that we can define the product vision, do the product research and make in-depth user analysis to identify all the market opportunities and threats - which smoothly leads us to the second stage.

Strategy

Business strategy is the foundation for the effective product design process. Before the development process begins it’s priceless to know if the product or service is really what people need.

The first reason for startup failure, according to CB Insights, is “no market needed” (42%). Next are run out of cash, not the right team, pricing issues or poor marketing. All of them can be eliminated by proper preparation = creating a business strategy.

Design

Knowing all the necessary basics like target group, users’ needs, users’ journeys and having all the useful tools such as sketches, wireframes, personas and surveys we can start the design phase.

The first part of the process is prototyping which helps to deliver a good user experience. It gives a chance to test an idea before building the full solution. The created prototype is then reviewed, refined and iterated. At the end of the prototyping phase, the design will be ready for production.

The next steps are testing and validating to make sure the design concept works as intended.

Development

The development phase is the final one. We start by choosing the right technologies, tools and workflow to achieve set goals. Depending on the requirements and project itself we can choose Jamstack development, PWA development, Ruby on Rails development, React development or other approaches. Then we set up the team of developers dedicated to your project and prepare the development schedule to make sure that the product will be ready just on time to conquer the market.
