Product Design

We start from understanding business’ and users’ needs, then we draw up an action plan and get to prototyping and development. When the product is tested, we show it to the world and wait for the feedback. Based on that, we can make the necessary changes and deliver a polished and profitable digital product.

Digital product creation is a complex process that can be divided into four stages, in accordance with the Double Diamond methodology: discover, define, develop, deliver.

New website for one of the biggest Polish banks
In September 2018 Eurobank has released the new version of their website. The complete project is a sum of the work of the specialist teams. One of them was Naturaily - the content-design team that was responsible for creating the information architecture of the website, as well as completely filling it with the right content and creating ready-to-implement CMS templates. The content for over 130 product and informative pages was prepared and implemented in just 2 months, as for the communication during the project - it was impeccable and just as agreed at the start of the cooperation.
