  • 01

    What are the benefits of custom ecommerce development?

    When you are just starting out, going for a ready-to-use ecommerce platform may sound like a good option, as it can save you time and money. In the worst case scenario though, it may cost your company in the future.

    Developing a custom ecommerce website tailored to your specific needs and processes can help you grow and scale your business in the long term.

    Some of the other benefits of using a custom ecommerce platform than an off the shelf solution:
    • Easy integration with your existing business systems
    • You can design your own unique transaction flow
    • Customization often improves conversion rates
    • Allows checkout process personalization
    • Enables additional customer service functionalities like order tracking, contacting your team, etc.
  • 02

    Will React.js work well for ecommerce projects?

    React.js was created by Facebook to handle the speed issues they had with technologies like PHP (the language that the extremely popular e-commerce platform Magento is based on). It can bring some additional advantages for an ecommerce website:
    • Flexibility: React is known for its component-based development, which lets developers reuse already written code and write small features.
    • Performance: React’s great advantage lies in how fast it performs in your app. It improves the user experience for your customers, as well as your conversions and SEO positioning, driving you more sales.
    • You can easily integrate it with a backend platform like Node.js, keeping all the code in the JavaScript for easy maintenance.
