  • 01

    What are the pros of Shopify?

    Shopify is being used by over 500k clients from all around the world. Here’s a couple of reasons why they choose it:
    • Ease of use
    • Storefront themes and designs
    • Built-in marketing tools
    • Excellent site performance
    • Great 24/7 support
    • Sales boosting features (like Abandoned cart recovery)
    • Over 100 payment processors supported
  • 02

    What are the cons of Shopify?

    No technology is perfect and, obviously, there are also a few downsides of the Shopify solution, some of them being:
    • Additional costs for some advanced features
    • Support limited for certain number of countries
    • No email hosting (however, there are dedicated Shopify email providers)
