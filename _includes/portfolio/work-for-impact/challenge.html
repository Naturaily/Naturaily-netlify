The challenge

Naturaily’s team, arm in arm with other specialists from Work for Impact’s team, had a serious challenge to face. They needed to develop a huge, scalable system with complex business logic. The need to match freelancers with suitable job offers was demanding, as there were various categories, skills and other variables.

Work for Impact is also meant to connect the organizations with the best professionals who will help them fulfill their mission. That made us seek a strong skill verification system that would let us ensure the quality of the service.
