How can JAMstack help my business grow?
JAMstack is in contrast to the traditional workflow that relies on monolithic CMSes like Wordpress or Drupal. By ditching the database, plugins and hosting maintenance, it can reduce operational and development costs.
A static site generator (SSG) is a tool that builds static pages out of the content you provide. It applies a selected template on it and generates a ready-to-use HTML website.
In contrast to traditional CMSes, which are hosted and served each time the request is made, the content in a headless CMS is decoupled. Therefore, it exists as a separate component and can be easily reused.
